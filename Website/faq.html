Equipment Questions

What types of tractors do you offer for small-scale farmers?

We offer a range of compact and utility tractors perfect for small-scale farming operations. Our selection includes models from 25HP to 75HP with various attachments suitable for different farming needs. These tractors are fuel-efficient, easy to maintain, and designed specifically for Pakistani farming conditions.

How often should I service my combine harvester?

We recommend servicing your combine harvester after every 150-200 hours of operation or at least once per harvesting season, whichever comes first. Regular maintenance should include engine oil changes, air filter cleaning/replacement, inspection of belts and chains, and thorough cleaning to remove crop residue.

What's the warranty period for your irrigation systems?

Our irrigation systems come with a 2-year comprehensive warranty covering all parts and labor. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and includes free maintenance for the first year. We also offer extended warranty options for an additional cost.

Can I get spare parts for older equipment models?

Yes, we maintain an extensive inventory of spare parts for equipment models dating back 15 years. If we don't have a specific part in stock, we can often source it within 2-4 weeks. We recommend contacting our parts department directly with your equipment model and serial number for the most accurate information.

Services Questions

Do you offer on-site equipment repair services?

Yes, we have a team of mobile technicians who can come to your farm for most repair and maintenance services. Our service covers a 100km radius around our main branches. For emergencies, we offer same-day service with a priority fee. Standard service calls are typically scheduled within 2-3 business days.

What's included in your annual maintenance contracts?

Our annual maintenance contracts include: 2 scheduled maintenance visits, priority service calls, 15% discount on parts, free equipment inspections, and 24/7 phone support. We offer different tiers of service contracts to match your equipment needs and budget.

How can I schedule equipment operator training?

Operator training can be scheduled through our customer service team by phone or email. We offer both group classes at our training centers and on-site individual training. New equipment purchases include 4 hours of complimentary training. Additional training sessions are available at reasonable rates.

Do you provide custom equipment modifications?

Yes, our engineering team can design and implement custom modifications for many of our equipment models. Common modifications include specialized attachments, control system upgrades, and performance enhancements. We begin with a consultation to understand your specific needs and provide a quote for the work.

General Questions

What payment options do you accept for equipment purchases?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and credit cards (Visa/MasterCard) for equipment purchases. We also offer financing options through partnerships with several Pakistani banks with competitive agricultural loan rates. Leasing options are available for certain equipment models as well.

How can I become a dealer for your products?

We're always interested in partnering with qualified dealers across Pakistan. Prospective dealers should have agricultural equipment experience, adequate facilities, and a strong local network. Please contact our dealer development department with your business details, location information, and proposed business plan.

Do you offer farming consultation services?

Yes, we have agricultural experts who provide consultation services on equipment selection, farm mechanization strategies, and operational efficiency improvements. Initial consultations are free, with more detailed planning services available at competitive rates. We also organize regular free workshops on modern farming techniques.

What safety certifications do your technicians hold?

All our technicians are certified by the relevant equipment manufacturers and hold Pakistan Engineering Council certifications where applicable. Our team undergoes regular safety training and is certified in first aid, electrical safety, and heavy equipment operation. We maintain strict safety protocols for all service and repair work.
