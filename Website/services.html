Service FAQs

Answers to common questions about our agricultural services

What areas of Pakistan do you serve?

We currently provide services throughout Punjab and parts of Sindh and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Our main service centers are in Lahore, Faisalabad, Multan, and Sahiwal, with mobile units that can reach surrounding areas. We're expanding to new regions each year - contact us to check availability in your area.

How quickly can you respond to equipment repairs?

For emergency repairs during critical farming periods, we guarantee a response within 24 hours in our primary service areas. Standard maintenance appointments are typically scheduled within 3-5 business days. Premium service contracts receive priority scheduling.

Do you provide training for equipment operators?

Yes, we offer comprehensive operator training programs for all equipment we sell or rent. Training includes both classroom instruction and hands-on field practice. We also provide safety certification that meets national agricultural standards. Training can be conducted at our centers or on your farm.

What makes your precision farming services different?

Our precision farming solutions are specifically adapted for Pakistani soil conditions, crops, and climate. Unlike generic systems, we customize every component - from soil sensors to irrigation controls - based on local requirements. We also provide ongoing data analysis and adjustments as conditions change.

Can I rent equipment for just one season?

Absolutely. We offer flexible rental terms from weekly to seasonal contracts. Our most popular option is seasonal rental that covers planting through harvest, including all maintenance. We can also provide operators if needed. Contact us for current availability and seasonal specials.
